DESIGN IMPROVEMENTS

What this redesign improves

The goal of this concept is to fix the biggest usability and catalog issues identified in the audit phase.

Clearer Product Pricing

Replaces confusing pricing patterns with better pack-size visibility, quote states, and cleaner labels.

Better Inventory Messaging

Buyers can quickly see whether items are in stock, running low, restocking soon, or quote-only.

Faster Ordering Workflow

Simplifies quantity handling and makes product discovery feel more natural for repeat B2B orders.

Stronger Category Structure

Reduces duplication confusion and gives buyers a cleaner way to navigate product families.

CHEF-CENTRIC HOMEPAGE

Built more like an operations tool than a generic storefront.

Professional buyers do not want clutter. They want speed, category confidence, and fewer surprises during ordering. This homepage concept puts quick actions, inventory signals, and B2B workflows first.
