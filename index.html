DYNAMIC INVENTORY ALERTS

How the homepage would handle live inventory communication.

Instead of hiding stock conditions inside product pages only, the redesign surfaces inventory states directly on the homepage and product cards. This supports faster chef decision-making during rush ordering and makes quote-only items clearer.

  • In Stock: ready for immediate ordering
  • Low Stock: prompts faster buying action
  • Quote Required: clearer alternative for market-price items
  • Restocking Soon: sets purchase expectations

THE PITCH

Why SAP Commerce Cloud is the right engine for this design.

This homepage is designed for a high-volume B2B distributor, not a simple retail store. SAP Commerce Cloud is the strongest fit because it supports enterprise-scale catalogs, custom B2B pricing logic, quote workflows, account-based ordering, and deep ERP integration.

That makes it the right platform to power chef-centric features like dynamic inventory alerts, premium category navigation, and operationally realistic purchasing experiences.

Scalability

Supports thousands of SKUs and high-volume B2B traffic with enterprise architecture.

Custom B2B Logic

ERP Integration
